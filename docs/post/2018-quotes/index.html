Dec 27, 2018 1 min read Inspiration
  • 23rd of June 2019

you should choose to enter by the small rivers, and not go right away into the sea, because you should move from easy things to difficult things.

(Letter of St. Thomas Aquinas to Brother John on How to Study)

  • 1st of March 2019

Apri la mente a quel ch’io ti paleso
e fermalvi entro; ché non fa scïenza,
sanza lo ritenere, avere inteso.

(Dante Alighieri, Divina Commedia, Paradiso V, 40-42)

As beautifully said in the famous terzina, there is no real knowledge without “stopping inside” and “retaining” what we have heard. Even more so in an age of incessant distraction and information overload.
